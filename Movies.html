2021 Movies Quiz - Movies

Be aware that this page may contain answers to some of the questions in the quiz. During the year of 2021 many successful movies were released such as Dune, Spider-Man: No Way Home, Shang-Chi, Raya and the Last Dragon, No time to die, Free Guy, the Tomorrow War, The King's Man, Chaos Walking, Jungle Cruise, Cyrano and Nightmare Alley.

My personal favourite movies of 2021 would have to be Dune, just slightly edging Spider-Man: No Way Home to the top spot. In third spot, Shang-Chi and the Legend of Ten Rings and closely behind that Raya and the Last Dragon. The final and fifth movie on my list is Free Guy, a fun action adventure movie starring Ryan Reynolds. Honourable mentions to the latest installment in the bond franchise (No Time to Die) as I have not personally watched it, Cruella as it is a fun movie which delves behind the curtain to a character we already know and love (or rather hate in some cases) and West Side Story for a thrilling re-imagination of a well known story.

Along with my personal favourites, my top five most disappointing movies of the year go to Encanto, due to its boring plot, The King's Man, which used the theatrics of the original without any real substance, and Eternals, a movie with good marketing that failed to live up to the hype.
